About 21ai

Founded in 2024, 21ai emerged from a simple observation: countless businesses were losing revenue due to slow response times and missed booking opportunities. Our team of AI specialists and business professionals came together with a mission to solve this problem using cutting-edge artificial intelligence and WhatsApp's ubiquitous messaging platform. We recognized that customers increasingly prefer WhatsApp for business communication, yet most booking systems hadn't adapted to this shift. 21ai was born from this insight, combining advanced AI technology with the messaging platform customers already love and use daily.

Our core mission is to empower businesses of all sizes with intelligent automation that feels human and personal. We believe that every business deserves access to enterprise-grade booking technology without the enterprise-grade complexity or cost. We're committed to continuously improving our platform based on customer feedback, staying at the forefront of AI innovation, and building a community of successful businesses that have embraced modern booking solutions. At 21ai, we're not just building software—we're building the future of customer engagement, one booking at a time.
